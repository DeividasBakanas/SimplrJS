Simple Controller Examples

You can use the verbs: ["view", "new", "update", "delete"] in your routes.

You trigger the commands via HashPart in the URL, or by calling Simplr.Controller.mRouteAndExecute().

Open the javascript console, and you can see the data associated with each command.
